💬 Bring This to Your Next 1:1

Your persona is not a performance review. It is a starting point for a curious conversation. Your manager's job in this 1:1 is not to fix the pattern — it is to understand the story underneath it. You get to decide how much you share and when.

If you choose to share, here are three questions worth bringing:

What does this result tell you about where I should focus my AI development right now?
What support or resources can you offer me from here?
How does my persona fit with what you are seeing across the team?

You do not have to share this report. But if you do, know that the goal of that conversation is understanding — not correction.

📊 Take the Quarterly Pulse

In 90 days, retake this assessment to see how your persona has shifted. Personas change as confidence, capacity, and context evolve — and watching that movement over time is often more valuable than any single result.

The personas in this assessment are meant to become shared language on your team — not labels, but a shorthand for conversations that might otherwise be hard to start. The more fluently your team can say "I am feeling pretty Overwhelmed Operator on this one" — the more honest and productive those conversations become.
