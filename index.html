📊 Take the Quarterly Pulse

In 90 days, retake this assessment to see how your persona has shifted. Personas change as confidence, capacity, and context evolve — and watching that movement over time is often more valuable than any single result.

The personas in this assessment are meant to become shared language on your team — not labels, but a shorthand for conversations that might otherwise be hard to start. The more fluently your team can say "I am feeling pretty Overwhelmed Operator on this one" — the more honest and productive those conversations become.

How to take a Pulse Check: Click Begin Assessment on the home page, fill in your details, and under Assessment Round select the quarter that applies — Q1 Pulse Check after your first 90 days, Q2 Pulse Check after your second, and so on up to Q6. Each pulse is 5 questions and takes about 3 minutes.
