If you feel any uncertainty, skepticism, or even quiet dread when AI comes up at work — that is not a flaw in your character. It is a completely human response to real change. This assessment is designed to meet you exactly where you are.

💬 Bring This to Your Next 1:1

Your persona is not a performance review. It is a starting point for a curious conversation. Your manager's job in this 1:1 is not to fix the pattern — it is to understand the story underneath it. You get to decide how much you share and when.

If you choose to share, here are three questions worth bringing. You do not need to ask all three — start with the one that feels most honest about where you are. The goal of this conversation is understanding on both sides.

I want to share what resonates in this result and what does not — because both tell you something real about where I am. The resonance shows where to build. The friction shows where to listen.
What would feel like a genuinely safe next step given where I am right now — one I could actually take this week, not one that sounds good in a conversation?
Can we define one specific thing I will try this month — and agree on how you will check in with me about it?

You do not have to share this report. But if you do, know that the goal of that conversation is not to close a gap — it is to take one honest step from wherever you actually are.
